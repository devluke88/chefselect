About Chef

Gabriel

A chef who would like to cook classic and modern for public. A vast experience in gastronomy, 18 years working in different locations like hotels, restaurants, pubs, bistros, glad to show to the people his abilities of cooking. Continuously willing to make people smiling with a fresh creativity and a fairy tale taste of the food and entertaining the public with successful events. Gabriel is able to do weddings, receptions, private parties with live cooking stations, live barbecues, buffets, take away finger food and canapes on a high level of cooking.
